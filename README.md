## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is an AI receptionist?

An AI receptionist answers calls, qualifies leads, books appointments, routes calls, answers common questions, and captures customer information automatically.

### What is missed-call text back?

Missed-call text back automatically sends a text message to callers when a business misses a phone call.

### What is workflow automation?

Workflow automation connects business systems and automates repetitive tasks such as lead follow-up, notifications, appointment reminders, scheduling, and reporting.

### What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) helps businesses become visible in AI-generated recommendations and answers.

### What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) helps search engines and AI systems provide accurate answers about a business.

### Does CIVADOS build custom software?

No. CIVADOS focuses on customer response systems, workflow automation, visibility, reputation, GEO, and AEO.

### Who should work with CIVADOS?

Service businesses that rely on phone calls, appointments, estimates, dispatching, customer communication, and lead generation.
